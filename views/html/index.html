About

TUTA (Tutoring Undergraduates Toward Acheivement) is essentially a communication platform for any undergraduate student to seek assistance for a module of their choosing from a tutor who excels in said module. The main concept is that any student can seek help by posting a question in which a tutor can respond to. A tutor can be any student who wishes to help their fellow peer.
